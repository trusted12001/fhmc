Services

Prayer Services

Prayer Services

We aims to provide a suitable and serene space for daily Salat, Salatul Jumu'ah, and Salatul Eid, ensuring that our members can worship in peace and comfort.

Tarawih Prayers

Tarawih Prayers

During the holy month of Ramadan, we organise Tarawih prayers, starting from the sighting of the new crescent moon until the last day of Ramadan.

Reminders

Reminders

We are committed to regularly reminding each other of Allah and the Hereafter, encouraging good deeds and preparing our souls for the journey beyond this life.

Halal Food Banks

Halal Food Banks

Our vision includes launching charity initiatives, such as Halal food banks, to support those in need within our community and beyond.

Janazah Services

Janazah Services

In times of loss, we seek to support each other by offering assistance with Janazah arrangements, conducted in accordance with Islamic teachings.

Education & Propagation

Education & Propagation

We plan to offer educational programmes and classes focused on Islamic teachings, aimed at deepening our understanding of the faith and preparing us for the Hereafter.

Community Engagements

Community Engagements

FHMC aspires to be a hub for social and spiritual engagement, offering guidance and facilities for engagements, weddings, and other important life events.

About Us

Farley Hill Muslim Community (FHMC) was established on the 19th April 2007 with a clear and noble mission: to enrich the lives of the Muslim Ummah in our community while fostering unity, support, and spiritual growth. We are dedicated to creating a welcoming environment where brothers and sisters can come together for worship, learning, and mutual assistance.

Our community is built on the principles of compassion, cooperation, and a commitment to the teachings of Islam. We strive to offer a space where everyone, regardless of background, feels a sense of belonging and can contribute to the betterment of our community and society at large.
